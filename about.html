About

This site was created by J. Calamia as an entry for the OpenDataPhilly Visualization Contest, hosted by Azavea. It uses datasets from SEPTA's Location API as well as data from the City of Philadelphia to determine bike routes, city boundaries, and rail locations. The site uses leaflet.js (for mapping), PureCSS (for styling), and GoogleCharts (for additional data visualization).
